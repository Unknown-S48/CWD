About the Timetable

The Timetable page is an essential tool designed to help students effectively manage their academic schedules. Our design focuses on user-friendliness, ensuring that students can quickly access and understand their weekly schedules. The use of responsive design techniques guarantees that the timetable remains functional and visually consistent across a range of devices, from desktop computers to mobile phones. This approach not only enhances accessibility but also supports a seamless and efficient scheduling experience for all users.
