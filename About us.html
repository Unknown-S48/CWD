The purpose

The purpose of CTU-Buddy is to create a central hub for educational resources, tools, and collaboration at City Technical University. By offering a range of interactive features—such as course materials, forums, academic tracking, and real-time feedback—the platform aims to support students' academic growth, facilitate meaningful engagement with course content, and streamline interactions within the university community.

The vision

To empower students at City Technical University with a cutting-edge, interactive educational platform that fosters a dynamic and engaging learning experience, setting a new standard in academic excellence and student success.

The mission

To design and implement CTU-Buddy as a comprehensive and user-friendly educational website that integrates innovative features and functionalities, enhancing the learning journey by providing personalized support, interactive resources, and seamless communication between students and educators.
